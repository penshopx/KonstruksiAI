## Questions to Personalize Your Project Design Document

To create a comprehensive and tailored Project Design Document that meets your specific needs, please provide details on the following:

### 1. Project Overview
- What is the **project name** and **location**?
- What type of project is this (e.g., residential, commercial, infrastructure, industrial)?
- What is the **project scope** and **primary objectives**?
- What is the estimated **project budget** and **timeline**?

### 2. Design Requirements
- What are the **functional requirements** of the project?
- What **technical specifications** must be met (e.g., structural loads, materials, systems)?
- Are there any specific **design standards or codes** that must be followed?
- What are the **sustainability or environmental requirements** (e.g., LEED, green building)?

### 3. Site Conditions
- What is the **site location** and **accessibility**?
- What are the **topographic and geotechnical conditions**?
- Are there any existing structures or **utilities** on site?
- What are the **environmental constraints** (e.g., flood zones, wetlands)?

### 4. Regulatory Requirements
- What **permits and approvals** are required from local authorities?
- Are there any **zoning or land use restrictions**?
- What **building codes and standards** apply to this project?
- Are there any **environmental impact assessment** requirements?

### 5. Stakeholders and Team
- Who is the **client** and what are their expectations?
- Who are the **key consultants** involved (e.g., architects, structural engineers)?
- What is the **procurement strategy** (e.g., design-bid-build, design-build)?
- Who are the **regulatory authorities** that will review and approve the design?
