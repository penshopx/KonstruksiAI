## 📋 Progress Report Questions

To create a comprehensive and tailored Progress Report for your construction project, please provide the following information:

### 1. Project Identification
- What is the name of the construction project?
- What is the project location (city, site address)?
- Who is the client/stakeholder receiving this report?

### 2. Project Timeline & Status
- What is the project start date and expected completion date?
- What is the current phase of construction (foundation, structural, finishing, etc.)?
- What is the overall percentage of completion?

### 3. Work Completed & In Progress
- What are the major tasks/activities completed since the last report?
- What tasks are currently in progress?
- What milestones have been achieved or are approaching?

### 4. Issues, Challenges & Risks
- What issues or challenges have arisen during this reporting period?
- Are there any delays, budget overruns, or scope changes?
- What potential risks have been identified?

### 5. Resources & Next Steps
- What is the current manpower/equipment status?
- What are the planned activities for the next reporting period?
- What support or decisions are needed from stakeholders?
