---

## 1. Project Overview

What is the project name, location (address/coordinates), and type of development (residential, commercial, industrial, infrastructure, mixed-use)?

What is the purpose of this site investigation (new construction, extension, renovation, change of use)?

---

## 2. Site Characteristics

What is the current topography of the site (flat, sloped, undulating)?

Are there any existing structures, utilities, or infrastructure on or adjacent to the site?

What is the approximate size of the site (area in sqm or hectares)?

---

## 3. Proposed Development

What type of structure(s) is/are planned (buildings, bridges, roads, towers, etc.)?

What are the anticipated foundation loads and types (shallow foundations, deep foundations, piles, rafts)?

What is the approximate number of floors and total building height/area?

---

## 4. Site History and Background

What is the previous use of the site (agricultural, industrial, residential, vacant, brownfield)?

Are there any known historical incidents, contamination, or geotechnical issues?

Is there any existing site investigation data, geotechnical reports, or as-built records available?

---

## 5. Investigation Scope and Constraints

What is the allocated budget for the site investigation?

What is the desired timeline for completing the investigation and receiving the report?

Are there any specific concerns or areas of interest that should be prioritized (e.g., slope stability, groundwater, contamination, seismic zone)?
