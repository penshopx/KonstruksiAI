## Rule 2: Questions for Clarification

To create the most accurate and useful Material Selection Report, please provide details on the following:

### 1️⃣ Project Application
What is the specific application or component for which materials are being selected? (e.g., structural beam, gear, pressure vessel, automotive part, aerospace component)

### 2️⃣ Operating Conditions
What are the expected service conditions?
- Temperature range (°C)
- Load type (static, dynamic, cyclic, impact)
- Environmental exposure (corrosive media, UV, humidity)
- Pressure conditions (if applicable)

### 3️⃣ Performance Requirements
What are the critical mechanical properties needed?
- Required strength (yield/tensile)
- Stiffness (modulus of elasticity)
- Toughness/ductility
- Wear resistance
- Thermal conductivity
- Other specific requirements

### 4️⃣ Manufacturing Constraints
What manufacturing processes are available or preferred?
- Casting, forging, machining, extrusion, additive manufacturing
- Surface treatment capabilities
- Assembly requirements

### 5️⃣ Cost & Availability Constraints
What are the budget considerations?
- Target cost per unit
- Production volume
- Geographic availability
- Sustainability requirements (recyclability, environmental impact)
