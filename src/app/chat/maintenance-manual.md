## Rule 2: Questions to Gather Requirements

### 1. Equipment/System Context 🔧
What specific equipment, machinery, or system does this Maintenance Manual need to cover? (e.g., industrial pump, HVAC system, conveyor belt, rotating equipment, manufacturing machinery, etc.)

### 2. Operating Environment & Conditions 🌡️
What are the operating conditions and environment where this equipment operates? (e.g., temperature ranges, humidity, dust, corrosive substances, continuous vs. intermittent operation, load conditions)

### 3. Maintenance Strategy Approach 📋
What maintenance approach do you prefer or currently use? (e.g., Reactive maintenance, Preventive maintenance, Predictive maintenance, Reliability-Centered Maintenance (RCM), Total Productive Maintenance (TPM))

### 5. Specific Requirements & Constraints 📝

## Key Reference Materials 📚

This Maintenance Manual will be crafted using insights from industry-leading references:

1. **Maintenance Planning and Scheduling Handbook** by Richard (Doc) Palmer (2013)
   - Comprehensive guide to maintenance planning and scheduling
   - Work identification, prioritization, and scheduling techniques
   - Preventive and predictive maintenance strategies
   - Documentation and data management best practices

2. **Reliability-Centered Maintenance** by John Moubray (1997)
   - Systematic approach to maintenance planning
   - Framework for identifying critical equipment
   - Failure modes and consequences analysis (FMEA)
   - Optimizing maintenance intervals

3. **The Maintenance Management Framework** by Anthony Kelly (2012)
   - Maintenance strategy development
   - Resource allocation and performance measurement
   - Alignment with business objectives
   - Maintenance management systems implementation
