## Questions to Personalize Your Health And Safety Plan

To create a comprehensive and tailored Health and Safety Plan that meets your project's specific needs, please provide details on the following:

### 1. Project Overview
- **Project Name**: What is the name of your construction project?
- **Project Location**: Where is the project located (city, region)?
- **Project Type**: What type of construction project is this? (e.g., residential building, commercial complex, infrastructure/road/bridge, industrial plant, mixed-use development)
- **Client/Owner**: Who is the client or project owner?
- **Project Value**: What is the estimated project value?

### 2. Project Scope and Duration
- **Scope of Work**: What are the main construction activities involved? (e.g., excavation, structural work, electrical, mechanical, roofing, interior finishing)
- **Project Duration**: What is the expected project timeline? (start date, completion date)
- **Workforce Size**: Approximately how many workers will be on site during peak construction?
- **Working Hours**: What are the planned working hours? (shift patterns, overtime expectations)

### 3. Site Conditions and Environment
- **Site Characteristics**: Describe the site conditions. (e.g., urban/dense area, rural/remote, near water bodies, constrained site, existing structures to work around)
- **Access and Logistics**: How will materials and equipment be delivered? Are there any access constraints?
- **Environmental Factors**: Are there any environmental concerns? (e.g., proximity to residential areas, noise restrictions, weather considerations, wildlife/vegetation protection)

### 4. Regulatory and Compliance Requirements
- **Applicable Standards**: Are there specific safety standards or regulations you need to comply with? (e.g., OSHA, local building codes, ISO 45001, company-specific standards)
- **Permits and Approvals**: What permits or approvals are required for health and safety?
- **Previous Incidents**: Are there any known previous safety incidents or concerns at this site?

### 5. Specific Safety Concerns and Priorities
- **High-Risk Activities**: Are there any particularly hazardous activities planned? (e.g., work at heights, excavation/deep foundation, heavy lifting, confined spaces, electrical work, demolition, temporary works)
- **Emergency Response**: What emergency response capabilities are required? (e.g., on-site medical facility, evacuation routes, emergency contact protocols)
- **Additional Priorities**: Are there any specific safety concerns or priorities you'd like to address?
