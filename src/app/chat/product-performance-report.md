Thank you for initiating this Product Performance Report project. To create a comprehensive and tailored report that meets your specific needs, I'll need some detailed information about your product and the context of the analysis.

### Question 1: Product Identification
What is the product you want to evaluate? Please provide:
- Product name and model number
- Product category (e.g., automotive component, industrial machinery, consumer appliance, etc.)
- Brief description of the product's function and intended use

### Question 2: Performance Metrics
Which performance metrics are most critical for your evaluation? Select from:
- **Reliability & Durability**: Failure rates, MTBF, service life
- **Efficiency & Output**: Power consumption, throughput, performance ratios
- **Quality & Precision**: Dimensional accuracy, surface finish, tolerance compliance
- **Safety & Compliance**: Safety factor, regulatory compliance, certification status
- **Cost & Manufacturability**: Production cost, defect rate, yield percentage

### Question 3: Testing & Data Availability
What testing has been conducted or data available?
- Have any prototype tests been performed? If yes, what type (functional, environmental, stress, etc.)?
- Is there historical field performance data available?
- Are there competitor products for benchmarking?

### Question 4: Target Audience
Who will be the primary readers of this report?
- Product Development Team
- Management / Executive Leadership
- Quality Assurance Department
- Regulatory Bodies
- Customer / External Stakeholders

### Question 5: Scope & Deliverables
What specific outcomes do you expect from this report?
- Detailed performance analysis with data visualization
- Root cause analysis of any identified issues
- Comparative analysis vs. competitors or previous versions
- Actionable recommendations for product improvement
- Cost-benefit analysis of proposed improvements
