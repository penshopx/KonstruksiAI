## Questions for Feasibility Study Development

### 1. Project Type and Purpose
What type of project are you proposing?
- Residential (single-family, multi-family, mixed-use development)
- Commercial (office building, retail center, hotel, warehouse)
- Industrial (manufacturing plant, processing facility, data center)
- Infrastructure (roads, bridges, utilities, transportation)
- Mixed-use or master-planned community

Please describe the general purpose and objectives of the proposed project.

---

### 2. Location and Site Conditions
Where is the project proposed?
- Geographic location (city, region, country)
- Site characteristics (size, topography, existing structures)
- Access and transportation infrastructure
- Adjacent land uses and community context

Are there any known site constraints or environmental concerns?

---

### 3. Technical Requirements and Scope
What are the key technical aspects of the project?
- Approximate size (square meters/feet, number of units/floors)
- Structural system and construction type
- Building systems (HVAC, electrical, plumbing)
- Special requirements (sustainability certifications, accessibility, safety)

---

### 4. Financial and Economic Considerations
What is the estimated project budget?
- Total investment amount
- Expected revenue or cost savings
- Funding sources (equity, debt, grants, public-private)
- Key economic indicators you want evaluated (ROI, payback period, NPV)

---

### 5. Timeline and Regulatory Framework
What is the project timeline?
- Desired start and completion dates
- Key milestones or deadlines
- Regulatory approvals required (zoning, environmental permits, building permits)
- Any phasing considerations
