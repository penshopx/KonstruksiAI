## Questions for Project Plan Development

### 1. Project Type and Scope
What type of construction project are you planning?
- Residential (single-family, multi-family, mixed-use)
- Commercial (office, retail, hospitality)
- Industrial (warehouse, manufacturing, processing)
- Infrastructure (roads, bridges, utilities)
- Institutional (education, healthcare, government)

Please describe the general scope and purpose of the project.

---

### 2. Project Location and Site Conditions
Where is the project located?
- Geographic region and country
- Urban, suburban, or rural setting
- Site characteristics (flat/rolling/mountainous, soil conditions, access restrictions)
- Climate considerations that may affect construction

---

### 3. Timeline and Key Milestones
What is your project timeline?
- Total project duration (start to completion)
- Key phases or milestones
- Any deadlines or constraints
- Is the project phased or single-phase?

---

### 4. Budget and Resource Constraints
What is the project budget?
- Total allocated budget
- Budget constraints or limitations
- Funding source and payment terms
- Any cost-saving requirements or value engineering expectations

---

### 5. Stakeholders and Team Structure
Who are the key stakeholders and team members?
- Project owner/client
- Main contractor
- Key subcontractors
- Consultants (architects, engineers)
- Regulatory authorities involved
